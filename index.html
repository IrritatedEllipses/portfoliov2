From Bartender to Full Stack Web Developer

Sometime in the mid 2000s I filled a glass with gin and a splash of tonic in exchange for money. I continued doing that for the next fifteen years. While making a personal connection with guests and solving their problems with wit and alcohol know-how has been fun I've been lacking something I crave in my life: A challenge. Development was a natural choice for me and, since the beginning of 2018 I've spent my time learning the ins and outs of front-end web development. Now I'm looking for a place to shake up my front-end cocktail while loading up my back-end rail.
